The Story

Gin opened the doors at 153a Bristol Road back in 2015 with a straightforward idea: run a garage the way garages used to be run. No clipboard of invented faults. No sharp intake of breath through the teeth. Just an honest look at what your car actually needs, a clear price before any spanner turns, and the kind of conversation you might have over a mug of tea rather than a service counter. A decade on, that idea hasn't changed — it's just gathered 71 five-star reviews along the way.

What makes this place different is harder to list than it is to feel. Walk in and you'll notice it: the tone is warm, the language is plain English, and everyone is welcome — whether you know your camshaft from your crankshaft or you just know the dashboard light shouldn't be on. Gin's team handles everything from routine MOTs and servicing to the work other garages would rather not touch: gearbox swaps, engine replacements, welding repairs, high-performance builds. They treat a ten-year-old hatchback with the same care as a weekend track car.

The pricing is competitive because the overheads are honest. There's no showroom to pay for, no franchise fee baked into your bill. Just a workshop that runs the way it should — efficiently, carefully, and with your MOT certificate or your keys back when promised. If something doesn't need replacing, they'll tell you. If it does, they'll explain why in words that make sense. That's the whole philosophy, and it's why people keep coming back.
