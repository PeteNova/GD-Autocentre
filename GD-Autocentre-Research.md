## About / Brand Story

GD Autocentre is an independent garage serving Gloucester and the surrounding areas. Established in 2015, with over 10 years of experience in the automotive industry, the team prides itself on providing a personal, honest service at competitive prices.

The garage describes itself as **"your local, friendly and reliable Car Repair and Maintenance Garage"**.

They are also **female-friendly** — promising to provide honest and straightforward feedback, explaining what needs to be done and why, without jargon or pressure.
